Airawati Rural Municipality No. 5 Dangwang, Baraula, Pakala, Bijli, Dhuwang (1-6, 9) and Raspurkot (1-5) are the districts of Pyuthan district. Residents looking for alternatives to the traditional agribusiness have started enjoying modern lifestyles. Apart from local students, other people from neighbouring rural municipalities also come to the educational institutions of this rural municipality for study. According to the 2011 census, the rural municipality has a population of 22,392, of which 12,727 are females (56.84%) and males are 9,665 (43.16%). The rural municipality is home to people belonging to different castes and religions.
